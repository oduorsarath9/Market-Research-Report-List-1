<p><strong>The Chart Plotter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GPS Based Chart Plotter</li><li>GNSS Based Chart Plotter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chart plotter market can be classified into two main types: GPS-based chart plotters and GNSS-based chart plotters. GPS-based chart plotters utilize the Global Positioning System to provide location and navigation data, primarily relying on signals from satellites. In contrast, GNSS-based chart plotters offer a broader range of positioning systems, including GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou, enhancing accuracy and reliability. Both types are essential for effective marine navigation and route planning, catering to various maritime activities and user needs.</p></p>
