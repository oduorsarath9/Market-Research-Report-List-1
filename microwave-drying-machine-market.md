<p><p>The microwave drying machine market primarily comprises two key types: microwave vacuum dryers and tunnel microwave dryers. Microwave vacuum dryers utilize a combination of microwave energy and reduced pressure to efficiently remove moisture from products, making them ideal for heat-sensitive materials. On the other hand, tunnel microwave dryers feature continuous processing, allowing products to move through a tunnel where they are exposed to microwave energy for uniform drying. Both types optimize drying times and preserve product quality, catering to various industrial applications.</p></p>
