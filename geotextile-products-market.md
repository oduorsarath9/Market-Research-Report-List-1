<p><ul><li>Woven Geotextile</li><li>Nonwoven Geotextile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geotextile products market is segmented into woven and nonwoven geotextiles. Woven geotextiles consist of interlaced fibers, providing high tensile strength and permeability, making them suitable for applications like reinforcement and separation in construction projects. Nonwoven geotextiles, on the other hand, are made from bonded fibers, offering excellent filtration and drainage properties, ideal for erosion control and landscape applications. Both types serve crucial roles in civil engineering, environmental protection, and infrastructure development, meeting diverse project needs.</p></p>
