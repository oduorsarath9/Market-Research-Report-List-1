<p><p>The railway wheels and axles market is primarily divided into two types: rolled and forged components. Rolled wheels and axles are produced through a process of deformation, resulting in high strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy loads and high-speed operations. In contrast, forged wheels and axles are created by shaping heated metal under pressure, offering superior toughness and resistance to wear and impact. Each type serves specific applications within the railway industry, ensuring operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
