<p><ul><li>Cloud-Based Patient Simulation Software</li><li>On-Premise Patient Simulation Software</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Patient simulation software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based patient simulation software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access simulations through the internet. This model offers flexibility, scalability, and reduced infrastructure costs, making it ideal for institutions that prefer minimal hardware investment. On-premise patient simulation software is installed and maintained on local servers within an organization. This option provides greater control over data security and customization, appealing to organizations with specific regulatory or compliance needs.</p></p>
