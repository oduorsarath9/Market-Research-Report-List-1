<p><ul><li>Diffraction Grating Interferometers</li><li>Vertical Scanning or Coherence Probe Interferometers</li><li>White Light Scatter-plate Interferometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The white light interferometric microscope market includes various types of instruments that utilize white light for high-resolution imaging. Diffraction grating interferometers employ diffraction patterns to analyze surface topographies. Vertical scanning or coherence probe interferometers measure variations in height by scanning vertically and using coherence techniques for precise imaging. White light scatter-plate interferometers focus on light scattering from surfaces, enabling observation of sub-micron features. Each type serves specific applications in materials science, biology, and nanotechnology for advanced surface characterization.</p></p>
