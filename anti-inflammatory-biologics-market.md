<p><p>The anti-inflammatory biologics market is categorized into two main types: synthetic and biological. Synthetic anti-inflammatory drugs, typically small molecules, are chemically produced and target specific pathways to reduce inflammation. In contrast, biological anti-inflammatories are derived from living organisms and include monoclonal antibodies, which target proteins involved in inflammatory processes. Both types are used in various conditions, such as autoimmune diseases, providing targeted treatment options that aim to minimize side effects while effectively controlling inflammation.</p></p>
