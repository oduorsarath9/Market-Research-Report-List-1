<p><ul><li>Heterogeneous Sheet Flooring</li><li>Homogeneous Sheet Flooring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sheet flooring market is primarily divided into two types: heterogeneous and homogeneous sheet flooring. Heterogeneous sheet flooring consists of multiple layers with varying materials, providing enhanced design options and superior comfort underfoot. It is typically used in residential and commercial settings where aesthetic appeal is crucial. In contrast, homogeneous sheet flooring is made from a single material throughout, offering high durability and ease of maintenance, making it ideal for high-traffic areas and environments requiring robust performance.</p></p>
