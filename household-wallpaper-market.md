<p><p>The household wallpaper market comprises three distinct types. Pure paper type wallpaper is made entirely of paper, offering a traditional look and easy customization, but can be less durable. Non-woven wallpaper blends natural and synthetic fibers, providing durability and ease of application, as it doesn't shrink or expand. Fiber type wallpaper, often made from textile materials, adds texture and luxury to interiors. Each type caters to different aesthetic preferences and practical needs, allowing consumers to choose based on style and functionality.</p></p>
