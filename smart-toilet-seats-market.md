<p><strong>The Smart Toilet Seats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Storage Heating</li><li>Instantaneous Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart toilet seats market features two primary heating types: storage heating and instantaneous heating. Storage heating systems maintain a reservoir of warm water, providing consistent temperatures for use during multiple flushes. This option is energy-efficient for households with frequent usage. Conversely, instantaneous heating systems heat water on demand, ensuring a continuous supply without the need for storing preheated water. This approach offers quick access to warmth, appealing to users prioritizing convenience and instant comfort in their bathroom experience.</p></p>
