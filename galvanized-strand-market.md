<p><strong>The Galvanized Strand Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electro Galvanized Strand</li><li>Hot-Dip Galvanized Strand</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Galvanized Strand market is categorized into two primary types: Electro Galvanized Strand and Hot-Dip Galvanized Strand. Electro Galvanized Strand involves a coating process where a thin layer of zinc is applied through electrolysis, offering moderate corrosion resistance and a smooth finish. In contrast, Hot-Dip Galvanized Strand is immersed in molten zinc, providing a thicker coating that enhances durability and corrosion resistance, making it suitable for harsher environments. Both types cater to various industrial applications requiring reinforced steel cables.</p></p>
