<p><p>The commercial pool cleaning robots market features two primary types: crawler drive and wheel drive robots. Crawler drive robots utilize tracks similar to those found on tank vehicles, providing superior traction and stability on various pool surfaces, especially uneven ones. In contrast, wheel drive robots use wheels for mobility, offering faster movement and easier navigation for cleaning larger, flatter areas. Both types are designed to enhance efficiency and reduce manual cleaning efforts in commercial pool maintenance settings.</p></p>
