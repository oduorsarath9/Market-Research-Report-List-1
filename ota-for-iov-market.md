<p><p>Over-The-Air (OTA) technology in the Internet of Vehicles (IoV) encompasses two primary market types: Firmware Over-The-Air (FOTA) and Software Over-The-Air (SOTA). FOTA focuses on updating the vehicle's embedded firmware, enhancing functions like engine performance and safety features. SOTA, on the other hand, deals with application software updates, improving user experience through navigation, infotainment, and connectivity apps. Together, these markets enable timely updates, enhance vehicle functionality, and maintain optimal performance without requiring physical intervention.</p></p>
